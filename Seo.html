SEO for Healthcare: 10 Ways to Reach Healthcare Consumers Through Search

Our expert team of writers, designers, analysts, and strategists manage the optimization efforts for hospitals around the country, helping them promote locations, doctors, and service lines — and acquire more patients.   • Understand your current SEO performance and identify opportunities for improvement through our popular SEO audits

  • Understand how mobile-first indexing and AMP are changing the playing field and ensure you are optimized to connect with mobile search users

  • Analyze your backlinks and develop a link building strategy

  • Manage the technical aspects that impact SEO, from developing effective HTML meta descriptions and manually shaping schema, to sitemap submissions and 404 resolution

  • Improve your local search efforts by implementing effective business listing strategies
